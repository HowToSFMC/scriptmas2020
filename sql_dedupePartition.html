/* Ever struggled to Dedupe on a single or multiple attributes? Try using a partitioning window in your SQL Query and you can choose not just what you want to dedupe your data on, but what priority you wish to dedupe against. Want to pick the first SubscriberKey that for an email address and a status from _Subscribers, try this */ select top 1 with ties subscriberkey ,status ,DateJoined from _Subscribers order by row_number() over (partition by emailaddress, status order by DateJoined asc)
